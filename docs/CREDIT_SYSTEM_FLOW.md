# Credit System Flow

## Overview
The credit system allows clients to pre-purchase credits that can be used for various services like day passes, meeting rooms, and custom items. Credits simplify billing and provide better value.

---

## Understanding Credits

### What is a Credit?
- **1 Credit = ₹500**
- Pre-paid currency for services
- Stored in your Credit Wallet
- Can be used across all services
- Tracked and audited

### Why Use Credits?
- **Convenience:** No payment gateway for each transaction
- **Better Value:** Bundled pricing, potential discounts
- **Simplified Billing:** One monthly invoice for excess usage
- **Budget Control:** Know your spending limits
- **Faster Transactions:** Instant deduction, no waiting

## Getting Credits

### 1. Contract Allocation (Monthly)
**What Happens:**
- Your contract includes allocated credits per month
- Example: 20 credits/month = ₹10,000 worth of services
- Auto-credited at start of each month
- Part of your monthly rent package

**Example Contract:**
- Monthly Rent: ₹50,000
- Allocated Credits: 20 credits (₹10,000 value)
- Total Package: ₹60,000/month

### 2. Initial Credits (One-time)
**At Contract Signing:**
- May receive initial credit grant
- Welcome bonus or promotional credits
- Added to wallet immediately
- Can use right away

### 3. Admin Grant (Special Cases)
**Scenarios:**
- Compensation for service issues
- Promotional campaigns
- Loyalty rewards
- Management approval

**Process:**
- Admin initiates credit grant
- Reason documented
- Credits added to wallet
- Notification sent to you

## Using Credits

### What Can You Buy?

#### 1. Day Passes
**Cost Example:**
- Day pass price: ₹1,180 (including GST)
- Credits needed: 3 credits (₹1,500)
- Excess: ₹320 (stays as credit balance)

#### 2. Meeting Room Bookings
**Cost Example:**
- 3-hour meeting: ₹2,360 (including GST)
- Credits needed: 5 credits (₹2,500)
- Excess: ₹140 (stays as credit balance)

#### 3. Custom Services
**Examples:**
- Printing: 2 credits per 100 pages
- Courier service: 1 credit per shipment
- Parking: 1 credit per day
- Pantry items: As per item pricing

### How Credits are Deducted:

**Calculation:**
```
Service Cost: ₹1,180
Credit Value: ₹500
Credits Needed: ₹1,180 ÷ ₹500 = 2.36
Rounded Up: 3 credits
Amount Deducted: 3 × ₹500 = ₹1,500
Excess Amount: ₹1,500 - ₹1,180 = ₹320
```

**Important:** Always rounded up to nearest whole credit

## Monthly Credit Cycle

### Beginning of Month:
1. **Allocated Credits Added**
   - Contract allocation credited
   - Example: +20 credits
   - Wallet balance updated

2. **Previous Month Reconciliation**
   - Calculate total credits used
   - Compare with allocated credits
   - Generate invoice if exceeded

### During the Month:
- Use credits for services
- Real-time balance updates
- Track usage in dashboard
- Alerts if running low

### End of Month:
1. **Usage Calculation**
   - Total credits used: 35 credits
   - Allocated credits: 20 credits
   - Exceeded by: 15 credits

2. **Invoice Generation**
   - Excess credits: 15
   - Amount: 15 × ₹500 = ₹7,500
   - GST (18%): ₹1,350
   - **Total Invoice: ₹8,850**

3. **Payment Due**
   - Invoice sent via email
   - Payment terms: 30 days
   - Multiple payment options
   - Late payment penalties apply

## Credit Transaction Types

### 1. Grant (Credits Added)
**When:**
- Monthly allocation
- Initial credits
- Admin compensation
- Promotional credits

**Effect:** Balance increases

### 2. Usage (Credits Deducted)
**When:**
- Day pass purchase
- Meeting room booking
- Custom service used
- Any credit-based transaction

**Effect:** Balance decreases

### 3. Deduct (Manual Removal)
**When:**
- Correction of error
- Penalty for violation
- Admin adjustment

**Effect:** Balance decreases

### 4. Refund (Credits Returned)
**When:**
- Service cancelled
- Booking refunded
- Overcharge correction

**Effect:** Balance increases

## Complete Credit Journey

```
Contract Activated
    ↓
Credit Wallet Created → Initial Credits Added
    ↓
Monthly Cycle Starts → Allocated Credits Added
    ↓
Member Uses Service → Check Credit Balance
    ↓
Sufficient Credits?
    ├─→ YES: Deduct Credits → Service Confirmed
    └─→ NO: Pay via Card/UPI → Service Confirmed
    ↓
Transaction Recorded → Balance Updated
    ↓
Month End → Calculate Usage
    ↓
Exceeded Allocation?
    ├─→ YES: Generate Invoice → Payment Due
    └─→ NO: No Additional Charge
    ↓
Next Month → Repeat Cycle
```

## Business Rules

1. **Minimum Balance:** No negative balance allowed
2. **Rounding:** Always up to nearest whole credit
3. **Non-Transferable:** Credits tied to your account
4. **Expiry:** As per contract terms (if applicable)
5. **Refunds:** Only for cancelled services
6. **Allocation Reset:** Monthly, doesn't carry forward
7. **Excess Billing:** Monthly invoice for overuse
8. **Payment Terms:** 30 days from invoice date
9. **Service Priority:** Credits used before direct payment
10. **Audit Trail:** All transactions logged

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: What happens to unused allocated credits?**
A: They don't carry forward. Use them within the month or lose them.

**Q: Can I buy extra credits in advance?**
A: Currently, no. Use allocated credits first, pay for excess later.

**Q: How do I know if I'm exceeding my allocation?**
A: Dashboard shows real-time usage and alerts when you exceed.

**Q: Can I share credits with another member?**
A: No, credits are tied to your client account.

**Q: What if I don't use any credits in a month?**
A: You still pay the monthly rent. Allocated credits are part of the package.

**Q: How are refunds handled?**
A: Cancelled services return credits to your wallet immediately.

**Q: Can I opt out of the credit system?**
A: Depends on your contract. Contact admin to discuss.

**Q: What happens if I don't pay the excess invoice?**
A: Late fees apply, and credit facility may be suspended.
